# Shinuyim (שינויים) — Israeli Modification Process

## Overview

Shinuyim is the process of requesting modifications to an off-plan apartment (דירה על הנייר) during construction. This is the primary window for affordable apartment customization in Israel.

## Timeline

```
Project Sales Launch
    │
    ├── Purchase agreement signed
    │   └── Modification request window opens
    │
    ├── 3-6 months before floor construction
    │   └── DEADLINE: Final modification requests due
    │
    ├── Floor construction begins
    │   └── Contractor implements approved modifications
    │
    ├── Construction complete
    │   └── Post-construction modifications (much more expensive)
    │
    └── Key handover (מסירת מפתח)
        └── Only cosmetic changes remain affordable
```

### Key Dates
- **Modification window**: Opens at purchase, closes 3-6 months before your floor is built
- **Typical construction timeline**: 2-4 years from sales launch to delivery
- **Decision pressure**: Buyers often have only 2-4 months to decide on changes
- **Cost deadline**: Changes requested after deadline cost 2-3x more

## Common Modification Requests

### Most Popular (by frequency)

1. **Open kitchen to salon** (פתיחת מטבח לסלון) — 70% of buyers request
   - Remove wall between kitchen and salon
   - Sometimes add island or breakfast bar
   - Usually easy (partition wall)

2. **Merge bedrooms** (איחוד חדרים) — 30% request
   - Combine two small bedrooms into one larger room
   - Common for couples without children wanting a home office

3. **Split large room** (פיצול חדר) — 20% request
   - Divide large bedroom for growing family
   - Requires adding wall + door + possibly electrical

4. **Expand master bathroom** (הרחבת חדר רחצה) — 25% request
   - Take space from adjacent bedroom/hallway
   - Move wall + reroute plumbing

5. **Enclose balcony** (סגירת מרפסת) — 15% request
   - Convert to indoor space
   - Requires permit (changes building envelope)

6. **Add electrical/data points** — 60% request
   - Extra outlets, TV points, network cables
   - Cheapest modification during construction

7. **Upgrade finishes** (שדרוג גמר) — 80% request
   - Better tiles, kitchen, bathroom fixtures
   - Not structural, just material upgrades

## Contractor Pricing Structure

Israeli contractors typically provide a price list (מחירון שינויים) that includes:

### Standard Items
```
Wall removal (partition):              5,000 - 15,000 ₪
Wall addition (partition):             3,000 - 10,000 ₪
Wall relocation:                       8,000 - 20,000 ₪
Door relocation:                       3,000 - 8,000 ₪
Door addition:                         5,000 - 12,000 ₪
Electrical point addition:             500 - 1,500 ₪
Plumbing point relocation:             2,000 - 5,000 ₪
Kitchen relocation:                    20,000 - 50,000 ₪
Bathroom addition:                     25,000 - 60,000 ₪
Upgrade tiles (per sqm above standard): 200 - 800 ₪/sqm
Kitchen upgrade (above standard):      15,000 - 50,000 ₪
Bathroom upgrade (above standard):     10,000 - 30,000 ₪
```

### Important Notes
- Prices are for DURING construction (shinuyim window)
- Same changes POST-delivery cost 2-3x more
- Contractor may refuse changes that affect adjacent apartments
- Some modifications may be offered only for specific apartment types

## Regulatory Triggers

### Changes That DO Require Tama Permit
- Modifying structural walls (any wall the contractor identifies as structural)
- Changing building envelope (enclosing balconies, moving exterior walls)
- Adding/moving windows or doors in exterior walls
- Changing apartment's designated purpose (residential ↔ commercial)
- Adding plumbing stacks (vertical pipe runs)
- Significant changes affecting fire safety

### Changes That DON'T Require Permit (Shinuyim Only)
- Moving or removing interior partition walls
- Changing interior door locations (within partitions)
- Moving electrical outlets and switches
- Upgrading finishes (tiles, paint, fixtures)
- Adding data/TV/phone outlets
- Kitchen layout changes (within same footprint)
- Built-in closets and shelving


## Common Gotchas

1. **Mamad is NEVER modifiable**: Even during construction. It's a civil defense requirement.
2. **Wet room stacking**: Bathrooms/kitchens must align vertically across floors. Moving plumbing horizontally has limits (~3m from stack).
3. **Fire regulations**: Apartment must maintain fire escape route. Can't eliminate required exit doors.
4. **Adjacent apartment impact**: Modifications can't affect shared walls or neighbor's layout.
5. **Elevator/stairwell walls**: Always structural, never modifiable.
6. **Mechanical rooms**: AC, electrical panels, water heaters have placement requirements.
7. **Price surprises**: Contractors may add surcharges for "unusual" requests not in standard price list.
8. **No guarantee of approval**: Contractor can refuse modifications, especially structural ones.

## Buyer Advice (for app tooltips)

- "בצע שינויים בשלב הבנייה — זול פי 2-3 מאחרי מסירה" (Make changes during construction — 2-3x cheaper than after delivery)
- "ממ״ד לא ניתן לשינוי בשום מצב" (Mamad cannot be modified under any circumstances)
- "פתיחת מטבח לסלון — השינוי הפופולרי ביותר" (Opening kitchen to salon — most popular modification)
- "בדוק עם הקבלן אילו שינויים אפשריים לפני ההחלטה" (Check with contractor which modifications are possible before deciding)
