## Rules
1. Every apt has EXACTLY 1 mamad
2. Plausible areas: bath<12, bed 8-25, salon 15-50, kitchen 6-20, mamad 9-15
3. Required rooms: bathroom, kitchen, mamad, salon
4. All rooms accessible via doors
5. Windows on exterior walls only
6. Envelope = single connected polygon, 50-200sqm
7. Dimensions consistent with geometry (±5%)
8. Furniture clearances: 80cm walk, 60cm chair, door swing clear
9. ALWAYS include structural disclaimer
